Privacy Policy

NSZ Goa Ride ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and the choices available to you. For privacy requests contact us at info@goaride.com or call +91 8262812997.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when you book or contact us (name, phone, email, booking details, pickup/drop locations). We may also collect payment-related information and documents needed to verify identity where applicable.

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website we automatically collect technical data (IP address, browser type, device identifiers, pages visited, and timestamps) using cookies, log files, and analytics tools to help improve the site and detect abuse.

How We Use Your Information

We use data to process and confirm bookings, communicate with you (including via WhatsApp), manage customer service, prevent fraud, and comply with legal obligations. We may use aggregated, non-identifiable data for analytics and business insights.

Sharing & Third Parties

We may share information with service providers who assist in booking, payments, or analytics. We do not sell personal data to third parties. We may disclose information to comply with legal processes or protect rights and safety.

Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes described, such as completing a booking, providing customer service, complying with legal obligations, and for legitimate business interests.

Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data. However, no online transmission is 100% secure. If you suspect a security issue, contact us immediately.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at info@goaride.com. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe as required by applicable law.

Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies for analytics and essential site functionality. You can control cookie preferences via your browser; disabling some cookies may affect site features.

Children

Our services are not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental consent.
